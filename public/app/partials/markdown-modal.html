Writing content

Monoture uses markdown to format and style your content. There are just a few simply rules to learn to get you started;

Headings

To write a heading, just start a new line with a hash (#). The number of hashes you use determine how big the header is;

# Heading 1 (Larger)
## Heading 2
### Heading 3
#### Heading 4
##### Heading 5
###### Heading 6 (Smaller)

Emphasis

Text can be italicised, bolded or both;

*This text will be italic*
_This will also be italic_
**This text will be bold**
__This will also be bold__
_You **can** combine them_

Lists

Lists are a great way to break up content. It's as simple as putting a star (*) for bullet lists or 1. for a numbered list;

* Bullet list item 1
* Bullet list item 2
* Bullet list item 3
1. Numbered list item 1
2. Numbered list item 2
3. Numbered list item 3

Images

Images are a little bit more tricky than headings and lists. They take the following format;

![alternative text](image url "Title text")
